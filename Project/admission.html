Admission Information

Admission to First Year Diploma in Engineering

The college runs Formal Diploma Courses of 3 year duration in various branches of Engineering viz Automobile, Civil, Computer Science, Electrical, Electronics, Information Technology, Mechanical, Printing. and 2-year Diploma course in Pharmacy.

The admission to these courses is done under direction and monitoring of Directorate of Technical Education, Satpura Bhavan, Bhopal. State Level PrePolytechnic Test (PPT) is conducted by VYAPAM (PEB) for 10th pass students in May-June every year. The complete process of Admission viz. Registration, Choice filing, Document verification, Allotment of seat, is online and executed by MPONLINE. Candidates can participate in the admission process in first, second or CLC rounds. They can visit www.dtemponline.com portal to have further information about Admission.

First round Allotment:

First round Allotment is done for admission on the basis of PPT merit score.

Second round Allotment:

Second round Allotment is done for admission on the basis of PPT merit score and 10th score.

CLC round Allotment:

The third round of admission is College Level Counselling (CLC) is organized from 10-15 August. Those students who, for some reason could not get admission in first and second round, have this opportunity to get seat in CLC round, subject to availability of seat.

Admission to First Year Diploma in Pharmacy

The college runs Formal Diploma Course of 2 year duration in Pharmacy. The admission to these courses is done under direction and monitoring of Directorate of Technical Education, Satpura Bhavan, Bhopal. Admission is given on the basis of 12th exam score. The complete process of Admission viz. Registration, Choice filing, Document verification, Allottment of seat, is online and executed by MPONLINE. Candidates who have passed 12th exam can participate in the admission process in first, second or CLC rounds. They can visit www.dtemponline.com portal to have further information about Admission.

Admission to Second year/ Third year Diploma in Engineering

Admission to Second year/ Third year is done on the basis of grades obtained in of Semester exams. The compulsory condition to get admission to higher semester is as per ordinance of RGPV.

Admission Fee Structure:

The tution fee for Diploma course is 10,000 Rs per year. There are other nominal fees viz. sports fee, placement fee, cycle stand fee etc. which makes a total approx. 10981/- Rs. The fee must be deposited from SBI Collect service on www.onlinesbi.com and the receipt must be submitted to Academic section to confirm admission. For more information visit page Fees Structure for Diploma.
